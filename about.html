About Us

We are a boutique law firm specializing in business transactions, corporate, and securities law. Our practice spans both private and public clients, and we provide hands-on and individualized attention for a wide range of legal needs—from simple business formation to complex securities work and mergers.

We are a small firm with a highly focused practice and are a general practice firm where we know we can be cost-effective and efficient. We provide value where larger firms may not.

Our firm prides itself on offering high-quality, tailored legal services with a business-minded approach. We understand each client’s goals and budget, and provide efficient, cost-effective legal strategies.
