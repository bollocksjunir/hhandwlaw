About Us

We are a boutique law firm specializing in business transactions, corporate, and securities law. Our lawyers possess significant experience in a wide array of matters and have advised numerous individuals and entities on corporate structures and strategies ranging from simple sales to more sophisticated valuation-based strategies.

We are a small firm with a highly focused practice and are not a general practice firm or large law firm. Accordingly, our office is selective in accepting matters where we are confident the client will receive exceptional value for services that are among the very best available.

The firm prides itself in offering excellent legal services and exceptional responsiveness at extremely competitive rates. We take a business-minded and strategic view of the law and are committed to providing the highest level of legal services with the individual attention clients expect from a boutique firm. In each matter, we strive to understand the issues and goals that our client presents, and we are committed to addressing each client’s needs in a cost-effective manner.
